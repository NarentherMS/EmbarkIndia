    Frequently asked questions

    What does it cost an institute?

    You pay the speaker's honorarium, which is agreed upfront before you confirm — no surprises after the lecture. Our matching service is free during early access.

    How quickly can we get a speaker?

    You'll have a shortlist within 48 hours of posting a need. End to end, most sessions are confirmed one to three weeks out, depending on the speaker's calendar and yours.

    How are speakers verified?

    We confirm identity and current employment — government ID plus a work email or LinkedIn with employment proof. We verify that people are who they say they are; your feedback after each session builds the quality record.

    What if the speaker isn't a fit for our batch?

    Say so at the shortlist stage and we'll send different names — that costs you nothing. If a confirmed session goes wrong, tell us in your review; it directly affects whether that speaker gets matched again.

    I'm a professional. What does joining cost me?

    Nothing. Listing, verification and invitations are free for professionals. You set your honorarium expectations during signup, and you can decline any invitation without penalty.

    Can we request a topic that isn't listed?

    Yes — the verticals are where our bench is deepest, not a boundary. Describe what you need in the form; if we can't match it well, we'll tell you honestly instead of forcing a fit.
