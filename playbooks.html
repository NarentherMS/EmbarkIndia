The problem

Most stream decisions are made by rumour.

Two years and twenty lakhs, steered by corridor wisdom. You've heard these:

Just take finance, it pays the most. — every uncle, every wedding
Marketing is for extroverts, ops is for engineers. — hostel corridor wisdom
CV points don't matter, placements are luck. — a senior who didn't convert
Decide later, keep your options open. — the advice that costs a year

Your MBA is too expensive for hearsay. Pick with a map instead.

What matters, when.

1
Before the MBA

Work retail shelves with your eyes: pick two brands and track everything they do for a month. Start a swipe file.

2
Term 1–2

Marketing club, but more importantly: win one live project with a real company. FMCG shortlists love evidence over enthusiasm.

3
Summer internship

FMCG summer = sales stint. Embrace the territory; the best PPOs go to people who loved the market, not survived it.

Questions, answered plainly

Frequently asked questions

Are the playbooks free?

Yes — fully free, no signup wall, no "unlock the rest" email gate. If anything ever costs money on Embark India, it will say so before you click, not after.

Who writes these?

The Embark team drafts them from conversations with practitioners in each stream, then keeps them updated as recruiting seasons change. They're living documents — spot something stale and we'll fix it.

I'm torn between two streams. What do I do?

Read the "this is for you if" section of both — written to help you rule out, not just rule in. Still tied? That's precisely the conversation mentorship is for; join the waitlist and we'll pair you with someone who has done both interviews.

Does my checklist progress sync across devices?

Not yet — ticks save in your browser on this device. Accounts with synced progress are on the roadmap; until then, use the device you study on.

Which streams are coming next?

Consulting and Product Management are already on the shelf as "coming soon", in that order. Want a different one prioritised? Tell us at hello@embarkindia.in — genuinely, the order is votable.

Can I contribute or correct something?

Please do. If you work in one of these streams and something reads wrong, write to hello@embarkindia.in. The playbooks get better the more practitioners argue with them.
