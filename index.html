How it works

One journey, five base camps.

The MBA path is a sequence, not a scramble. We walk it with you stage by stage — and you always know which camp you're at.

Stage 1

Discover

A frank profile evaluation with a mentor: your academics, work experience, goals — and which schools genuinely fit them.

1
Stage 2

Prepare

A study plan built around your CAT, XAT or GMAT date — with mentor check-ins that keep the plan honest when work gets busy.

2
Stage 3

Apply

Essays, SOPs and forms that sound like you on your best day — drafted with editors who know what each school reads for.

3
Stage 4

Interview

Mock WAT-PI panels with alumni of your target schools, followed by written feedback you can act on before the real thing.

4
Stage 5

Admit

Offers in hand, we help you compare programmes, negotiate scholarships and get ready for day one on campus.

Alumni mentorship

Essays & interviews

SOP and essay editing plus mock WAT-PI panels, with written feedback after every session so progress is visible.
